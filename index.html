Our Donate Plan

Our Sylhet Aid team will handout relief packages to affect homes which includes food packs and boats for essential travelling.We plan to help the most vulnerable people to access nutritious food and essential items as soon as possible.

Food For a family

Our programmes go beyond traditional aid models to beat hunger in innovative ways. Increasingly, we send cash directly to hungry families because they are the ones who know best how to end their hunger for good.

Shelter for a family

We work with people to access the services they need to move towards recovery.Our service includes advocacy,advice,support and more to enable each person to achieve their goals and get back on their feet.

Education for a child

Educate for Life works to ensure integrated provision of education.Educate for Life works in partnership with local organisations, providing a range of services to our beneficiaries.

Community Health Outreach Work

We have a small health and community team,including a member of the local community,which has built strong relationships with Bakhel community members.This has led to changes in the health seeking behaviours and practices of the community,including a significant increase in community members choosing to access professional healthcare.
